Featured Temple

Seattle

Plans to construct the Seattle temple were announced 15 November 1975, Church leaders broke ground 27 May 1978, and Spencer W. Kimball, president of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints from 1973 to 1985, dedicated the Seattle Washington Temple 17 November 1980. The temple serves Latter-day Saints in western Washington.

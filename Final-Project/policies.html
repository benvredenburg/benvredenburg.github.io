Guest Policies

All guests are welcome at Temple Inn & Suites, whether they are members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints or not, provided that they adhere to our policies in keeping with Church standards, such as the following:

— Appropriate language: No use of profanity or vulgar language on hotel property.

— No smoking or drinking / possession of alcohol or illegal drugs on hotel property.

— Moderate dress: Dress code adheres to standards set for missionaries of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, with some exception. Tattoos, multiple piercings, beards, and unusual hair colours are permitted for guests.

For more information please visit Missionary Style Guidelines.

Temple Inn & Suites reserves the right to discontinue service to guests who do not follow the policies as outlined if the guest's behavior becomes a distraction to the overall message and mission of our hotel. Such guests understand that they will not receive a refund if removed from the premesis for disruptive behavior.
